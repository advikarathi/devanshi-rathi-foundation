Ayush Jaiswal DRF Scholar 2018 & 2019

"Hi, I am Ayush, the winner of the D.R.F. scholarship of 2018 and 2019. This award gave me the motivation to work harder for my best future interests. The scholarship is an excellent platform for the students to enhance their skills."

– Ayush Jaiswal '18 & '19 winner and full merit scholarship recipient at Ashoka University under DRF mentorship

Neeraj Sharma DRF Scholar 2019

"I am Neeraj Sharma, a student in grade 9, originally from Bholpur, Rajasthan. This prize boosted my confidence and also gave me a lot of motivation to succeed further in my goals. Chess is a game that not only develops my mind but also helps me build my own strategies."

– Neeraj Sharma '19 winner

Mrunali Pande Project Checkmate learner

"I learnt about DRF from my friends. I am amazed at the drive shown by the organisation towards hosting impactful events and sessions. I look forward to working with the foundation and growing as a chess player."

– Mrunali Pande, Project Checkmate learner and chess player
