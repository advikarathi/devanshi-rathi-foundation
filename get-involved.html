01 — Donate & Support

Support our work — and claim your 80G deduction.

The DR Foundation is 80G certified under the Income Tax Act, 1961 — meaning your donation is tax-deductible in India. Every rupee goes directly toward chess training, scholarships, and health programmes for underserved youth.

80G Certified · Tax-deductible donations
1

Use the contact form to reach us, selecting “Support enquiry” as the reason.

2

We’ll share bank transfer details and issue an 80G receipt for your records.

3

Corporate donors receive a CSR impact report and co-branding opportunities.

Chess & Education

Contributes toward chess training materials, tournament entry fees, and educational resources for visually impaired students at NAB, New Delhi.

Scholarship Fund

Contributes toward cash prizes and year-long mentorship for scholars with disabilities aged 15–30 across India.

Health Programmes

Supports dental camps, menstrual hygiene education, mental health sessions, and nutritional support at partner NGOs across India.

02 — Volunteer & Intern

Join the team

DRF welcomes volunteers and interns from any background — whether you’re a student, recent graduate, or working professional. All positions are remote and flexible, with on-ground opportunities available for those based in Delhi.

You won’t receive a salary or stipend, but you will receive an e-letter of recommendation on completion, and the chance to contribute meaningfully to a cause that matters.
