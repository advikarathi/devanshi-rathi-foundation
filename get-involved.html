01 — Donate

Your contribution
changes a life.

Every donation to the DR Foundation goes directly toward scholarships, chess training, and health initiatives for underprivileged and disabled youth across India.

All donations are 80G tax-exempt under the Income Tax Act, 1961

Scholarship fund

Health programmes

Supports dental camps, menstrual hygiene education, mental health sessions, and nutritional support at partner NGOs.

02 — Volunteer & Intern

Join the team

DRF welcomes volunteers and interns from any background — whether you’re a student, recent graduate, or working professional. All positions are remote and flexible, with on-ground opportunities available for those based in Delhi.

You won’t receive a salary or stipend, but you will receive an e-letter of recommendation on completion, and the chance to contribute meaningfully to a cause that matters.
